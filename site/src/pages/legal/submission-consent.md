# Story Submission Consent

> Draft for founder review. Not legal advice. A lawyer should check this before it goes live.

**Plain summary (three lines):**
This is the notice you see and agree to before you send us your story.
It says what happens to your story, what we strip out before showing it, and that you can pull it back.
Sending a story does not make us your lawyer, and you confirm the story is yours and true as you know it.

## Consent text shown at the story form

Read this before you submit. By submitting, you agree to it.

**What happens to my story.** I am sending my story so it can help show a pattern. A person on the team reads it before anything is published. It is not posted automatically.

**Who reads it.** The team reads it during review. If facts need checking, a reviewer may look at it more closely.

**What gets removed before it is shown.** Before my story is displayed, the team scrubs out anything that identifies me. That includes my name, account numbers, my exact address, and other identifying details. If a location is shown, it is only at the city, state, or zip level. Never my exact address.

**Contact and verification.** If I choose to give contact info, the team may contact me to check facts. Giving contact info is optional. My contact info is never displayed. It is not shown with my story or anywhere else on the site.

**Withdrawing my story.** I can withdraw my story at any time. If I ask, the team takes it off the site. The team keeps an internal copy for its records, and that copy is never displayed or shared.

**No lawyer or agent relationship.** Submitting my story does not make anyone here my lawyer or my agent. No one here represents me. If I have my own dispute, I handle it as myself.

**My promise about the story.** I confirm this story is mine. I confirm it is true to the best of my knowledge.

**My age.** I confirm I am at least 18 years old.

**I agree.** By submitting, I confirm I have read this and I agree to it.
