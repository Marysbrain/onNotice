# Privacy Policy

> Draft for founder review. Not legal advice. A lawyer should check this before it goes live.

**Plain summary (three lines):**
We collect almost nothing when you browse. There are no tracking cookies.
If you send us a story, you choose to, and we scrub out anything that identifies you before we show it.
We never sell your data, build profiles on people, or follow you around other sites.

## When you just read the site

You do not need an account to read anything here.

We do not set tracking cookies. We do not track you across other websites.

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to count things like page views. It does not use cookies and does not track you from site to site. It gives us rough, aggregate numbers so we know what pages people read. It does not tell us who you are.

The site is hosted on Cloudflare. Like any web host, Cloudflare handles the basic traffic needed to load a page. We do not add our own tracking on top of that.

## When you submit a story

Sending us a story is your choice. You do it by filling out the story form.

Before you submit, you see a consent notice that explains what happens next. You have to agree to it.

Here is what we do with your story:

- We read it before anything is published. A person reviews it.
- We scrub out things that identify you. That means names, account numbers, exact street addresses, and other identifying details.
- We only show location at the city, state, or zip level. Never your exact address.
- If you give us contact info, we may use it to check facts with you. We never display your contact info. Ever.
- We store your story and its records so we can stand behind what we publish.

## Turnstile on the story form

The story form uses Cloudflare Turnstile. Turnstile is a spam and bot check. It helps make sure a real person is submitting, not an automated flood.

Turnstile is not a tracking cookie and is not there to profile you. It runs only on the form. It checks that the submission is human and then gets out of the way.

## Removing your story

You can ask us to remove your own story at any time. Tell us and we take it down from the site.

When a story is withdrawn, it comes off the site. We keep an internal copy in our records log. We do that so we can stand behind what we published if it is ever disputed. The internal copy is never displayed again and is never shared.

## What we never do

- We never sell your data.
- We never build a profile on any individual person.
- We never track you into other websites.
- We never show your contact info.
- We never publish your exact address.

## The data we show on the site

Most of what you see on this site comes from public sources. That includes things like FCC and FTC records, court filings, arbitration disclosures, SEC filings, and public social posts shown only in aggregate.

We keep a public methodology page that explains where our data comes from and how we handle it.

## Changes

This policy can change. When it does, we date the change. If a change is substantial, we note it.
