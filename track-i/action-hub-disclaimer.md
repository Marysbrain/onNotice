## General information only

The take action hub gives you general information. It links you to places like FCC complaint forms, state attorney general forms, and carrier dispute channels.

This is not legal advice. It is not advice about your specific situation.

## No lawyer relationship

Using this hub does not make us your lawyer. We do not represent you. We are not your agent.

## No guaranteed outcome

Filing a complaint or a dispute does not guarantee any result. We cannot promise what an agency, a court, or a carrier will do.

## Use your own facts, in your own words

When you file, describe what actually happened to you. Use your own words. Do not copy someone else's story.

Your own honest account is what matters. State the facts you know to be true.

## Where to find real legal help

If you need actual legal advice, talk to a lawyer.

- Your state bar usually runs a lawyer referral service. Search for your state bar and "lawyer referral."
- Legal aid may help if cost is a concern. Search for "legal aid" and your state or county.

We do not recommend any specific law firm. Choose who you trust.
